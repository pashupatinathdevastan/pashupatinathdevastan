About Lord Pashupatinath

Om Namah Shivaya Dear Devotee,

You are warmly invited to visit the sacred Pashupatinath Temple in Shivabasava Nagar, Belagavi, one of the most revered shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva, the protector of all beings. This ancient temple is a divine sanctuary where the power of Lord Pashupatinath is felt by every soul who enters.

Join us in worship, meditation, and celebration within these sacred grounds. Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of the Shiva linga, the chanting of holy mantras. Come witness the daily rituals, evening aarti, and immerse yourself in the sacred ambiance where sadhus, priests, and pilgrims gather in devotion. Let the presence of Lord Pashupatinath fill your heart with peace, guidance, and blessings.

Whether you come to seek spiritual solace, offer prayers, or simply experience the powerful energy that flows through these holy grounds, may your journey to Pashupatinath be filled with grace and divine blessings.

We look forward to welcoming you to the abode of Lord Pashupatinath.

Har Har Mahadev!


With warmth and devotion, Pashupatinath Temple Committee
